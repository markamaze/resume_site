About Me

It is my goal to be productive in my persuits and continually grow in all aspects of what I do and who I am.

To succeed I will
  • gather information and ask questions
  • apply prior knowledge and experiences
  • consider multiple viewpoints
  • evaluate possibilities, top-down and bottom-up
  • have a willingness to work through adversity
  • maintain an open-mindset and allow for error
  • keep focus on the desired outcome

I will try to do this in all I do; whether writing software, building an over-engineered something, working within my role, repairing an old boat, or when my five year old concludes that Pluto is no longer considered a planet because scientists learned it is actually a dog.

Software Developer

I really enjoy writing software. I started coding after asking myself what I tend to do when I have no other obligations; to which the answer was to get on my computer. Contributing to the usefulness of technology as a tool, by writing software, is where I hope my career takes me.

Builder/Engineer Hobbiest

I love to create functional solutions. For example, when in college once, I needed a desk but only had a small car, a 6 hour drive, everything I owned, and temporary access to a shop. So i designed and built a desk in multiple pieces that bolted together and fit in the back seat. Used it for years.

Forever a Student

Learning is something I will never stop doing. I am willing to explore and seek understanding in anything.
