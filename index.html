About Me

There are many ways in which technology could help to better use our resources, and I want to work to help conceptualize and build software for it.

It is from my previous employment experience that I realized my interest in programming when it was apparent that a major aspect of my job was very programatic. That part of my job occupied much of my time, time which could be better spent. As my excitement grew working out the data structures which could carry the business logic, I knew I had found the career change I'd been looking for.

I used the concept as a learning project to begin my path to a new career. With prior experience studying engineering and some computer science, I worked to build my designs with a focus on gaining solid understanding of quality software development.

Mind Your Business

My first project, built as a means to learn about software development, is a tool which is meant to automate custom business operations for custom organizational models. Supported business operations (communication, documentation, training, etc) are created and defined, in order to be subscribed to by any part of the organization. Subscriptions are then monitored for compliance by the server, and automate the required actions based on the state of the subscription.

Fudget Budget

Fudget Budget is a personal budgeting tool built for Android. Enter income & expense transactions and the frequency they occur, and those are projected out to calculate your future account balance with each transaction.
